Feel like one sport isn’t enough? Look no further! The UBC Thunderbirds Triathlon Sport Club is for anyone who is committed to training for all three disciplines and competing at a high level in the sport.

We take advantage of the unique inclusive character of triathlon. Athletes on our team come from diverse sports backgrounds. Some of our athletes join the team as experienced triathletes, but many come from other sports and translate their athleticism into speedy swim, bike, and run performances.

Tryouts

Tryouts are held each September. You must attend one swim, one bike, and one run tryout.

  • Swim: 400m timed pool swim
  • Bike: Timed hill climb and flat effort (same session)
  • Run: 4km timed road run

Eligibility Requirements

We welcome committed athletes regardless of prior triathlon experience. What matters most is dedication, positivity, and team spirit.

Equipment Requirements

  • Running gear
  • Road or TT bike, cycling gear (indoor trainer encouraged)
  • Swimming gear (cap, goggles, pull buoy)

Athlete Expectations

  • Attend at least 4 practices/week (1 per discipline + 1 strength)
  • Race in UBC Triathlon in March and 1 additional major event

Academic Requirements

  • Maintain good academic standing (≥ 60% average)
  • Be enrolled in at least 9 credits/term or in a graduate/PhD/co-op program
  • Note: Students in non-UBC programs (e.g., Regent, Carey) are not eligible

Team Fees

  • $250 per term (covers coaching, facilities, partial competition costs)
  • $75 annual TSC fee (includes gear, insurance, banquet ticket)
