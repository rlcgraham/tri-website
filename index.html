About the Team

The UBC Thunderbirds Triathlon Sport Club is a group of highly motivated student-athletes pursuing high performance in the sport of triathlon while attending school full-time at the University of British Columbia. We come from all different academic and athletic backgrounds but are united by our love for swim-bike-run and our drive to achieve our full potential in sports and elsewhere. To join our mailing list and stay up-to-date on all of the latest tryout info, fill out our TSC Prospective Athlete Form! Tryouts for the 2025-26 season will be held in September 2025. See the how to join tab for more information.
